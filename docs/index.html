Environmentalist Computer Programmer

In our age of information, we live in a world where digital technology affects every part of our society. From protecting health information of patients to delivering food to those in need, this is a medium that has and will continue to change our world. So far, however, digital technology hasn’t created the cooperative and sustainable future that it can very easily achieve. My goal is to use computer science and computer vision to create this future.

Since I was 16, I have been learning about computer vision and convolutional neural networks. Creating Artificial Intelligence that can democratize powerful skills is important to me, and computer vision is a domain within machine learning that has the potential to revolutionize healthcare. The changes I’m most involved with are in the application of computer vision/machine learning for cancer detection. I am confident that I can change our world of healthcare by continuing to research and develop my skin cancer detection app and becoming an expert in computer vision, creating unbiased solutions that are accessible to everyone.
